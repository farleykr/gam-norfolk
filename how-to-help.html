How to Help

Volunteers are important to Ghent Area Ministry

We have flexible hours to suit your schedule. You can help by...

  • making the office function efficiently (answer phones, greet clients)
  • keeping the technical side functioning properly (computers)
  • sorting and tagging clothes in the coat closet
  • keeping our building/grounds well-maintained

Donations are always welcome and are important to Ghent Area Ministry

We take donations of...

  • Gently-used clothing for men & women
  • Lightweight jackets, jeans, Docker style pants
  • White crew-style work socks and men's underwear, briefs, and t-shirts
